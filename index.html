My research interests

  • Contents: My research activity is placed in the wide discipline of statistical-mechanics methods for complex systems analysis, particularly concerning spin-glass theory and its application to Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning.
  • Methods: Development of rigorous techniques towards a solid-grounded mathematical theory of AI and ML.
  • Other interests: Statistical inference for biological problems, including heart rate variability and effects of chemiotherapeutic drugs.

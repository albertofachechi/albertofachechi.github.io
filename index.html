My research interests

  • Contents: My research activity is placed in the wide discipline of statistical-mechanics methods for complex systems analysis, particularly concerning spin-glass theory and its application to modern Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning.
  • Methods: A crucial point of my research is the development of rigorous techniques towards a solid-grounded mathematical theory of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning.
  • Other interests: I also dealt with statistical inference for biological problems, particularly concerning non-linear analysis for heart rate variability.

Additional information

  • Since April 2023, I have been part of the FAIR foundation, a PNRR-funded project aiming at high-quality research in the field of Artificial Intelligence.
  • My recent research activity has benefited from the stimulating environment provided by the Alan Turing Institute’s event “Physics-informed Machine Learning”.
  • My work on "Dreaming Neural Networks" has attracted much attention from the general press, see for example this article.
